DID YOU KNEW THAT

THE WORLD WE LIVE IN TODAY IS STRUCK BY SEVERAL CRISIS: HEALTH, FINANCIAL, MORAL, SOCIAL, IDENTITY, ETC.

AMIDST ALL THESE CRISIS, THE LOVE OF GOD FOR US REMAINS OUR ONLY SOLACE, FOR IT IS THROUGH THE LOVE OF GOD THAT WE HAVE ACCESS TO ETERNAL LIFE.

JOHN 3:16 FOR GOD SO LOVED THE WORLD THAT HE GAVE HIS ONE AND ONLY SON, THAT WHOEVER BELIEVES IN HIM SHALL NOT PERISH BUT HAVE ETERNAL LIFE.

GOD WANTS ALL OF US TO HAVE ETERNAL LIFE, BUT THIS IS ONLY POSSIBLE THROUGH FAITH IN JESUS CHRIST.

BUT HOW WILL I RECEIVE ETERNAL LIFE?
1

ACCEPTE BEFORE GOD THAT YOU ARE A sinner AND INCAPABLE OF SAVING YOURSELF.

Romans 3:23 For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God; We can never pay the penalty for all our sins, nevertheless, God has provided a way for us to be forgiven so that we may have access to eternal life.

2

CONFESS YOUR SINS BEFORE GOD AND ASK HIM TO FORGIVE YOU COMPLETELY.

1 John 1:9 If we confess our sins, God is faithful and just and, consequently, he will forgive us our sins and purify us from all unrighteousness.

3

BELIEVE IN YOUR HEART THAT JESUS CAME, DIED, WAS BURIED, AND WAS RAISED FROM THE DEAD TO SAVE YOU AND GIVE YOU ETERNAL LIFE.

Romans 5:1 Therefore, since we have been justified by faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ.

4

RECEIVE BY FAITH!

Ask Jesus-Christ to come into your heart as Lord and Savior to have eternal life.

We receive eternal life by opening our hearts to Jesus and accepting Him as Lord and Savior.

Jesus declared: "I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me." (John 14:6)

He came to earth, shed His blood, and died on the cross for your sins. Through faith, you can be forgiven and receive eternal life for free, because He paid the price for your salvation. By accepting Him in your heart, His blood cleanses all your sins, and you become righteous beforeGod. (Romans 4:7)
